Meet Ritika Matta

Ritika Matta | Therapist

Mental Health Service Specialist

Therapist | Facilitator | Writer | Trauma-Informed Practitioner

I am here for you if you find yourself caught up in the midst of crisis and need therapy sessions or workshops. With extensive experience in trauma-informed care and evidence-based therapeutic interventions, I create a safe, nurturing space where authentic healing can begin and meaningful relationships can flourish.

My practice is built on the understanding that childhood experiences profoundly shape our adult relationships, emotional patterns, and overall well-being. Through personalized therapy sessions, transformative workshops, and insightful therapeutic writing, I help individuals navigate their emotional landscapes, process unresolved trauma, develop healthy coping strategies, and build deeper, more authentic connections with themselves and others.

My Therapeutic Approach

  • Trauma-Informed Care: Understanding how past experiences impact present-day functioning
  • Integrative Methods: Combining cognitive-behavioral, humanistic, and somatic approaches
  • Strength-Based Focus: Building on your inherent resilience and natural healing capacity
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Respecting diverse backgrounds and belief systems
  • Collaborative Partnership: Working together as equals in your healing journey
  • Holistic Wellness: Addressing mind, body, and spirit for complete healing
